WHAT IS CTF?

Capture the flag (CTF) is the cyber security competitions.
CTF can help beginners get an idea about the world of cybersecurity and help experts practice their skills.

Binary Exploitation

Binary Exploitation is to change behaviour of the binary or crash the binary to get access.

Reverse Engineering

Reverse engineering is the art of disassembly the compiled program and analysis the program's function and structure.

Cryptography

Cryptography is the study of secure communications techniques that allow only the sender and intended recipient of a message to view its contents.

Forensic

Forensic is the people who collect, process, preserve, and analyze computer-related evidence. They help identify network vulnerabilities and then develop ways to mitigate them.

Android Exploitation

Android Exploitation is securing the app from malware, digital frauds and other crimeinals.

Web Exploitation

Web exploitation is a common way of attacking websites.
When hacker exploit vulnerabilities in coding to gain access to a server or database.
